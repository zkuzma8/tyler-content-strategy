Script 7: Winter Tire Pressure Myth

30 seconds | Tips

[0-3s] HOOK:

"Your tire pressure light came on this morning? Here's what's actually happening."

[3-22s] CONTENT:

"Cold air is DENSER than warm air. So when temperatures drop, your tire pressure drops too - about 1 PSI for every 10 degrees. That doesn't mean you have a leak. It's just physics. Check your pressure when tires are COLD, fill to the number on your door jamb - NOT on the tire."

[22-27s] PAYOFF:

Filming Tips

ElementRecommendation
EquipmentiPhone is sufficient. No professional gear needed.
LightingFace natural light (window or outdoors). Never backlit.
AudioQuiet location. Wind ruins audio.
FormatVertical (9:16) for all platforms
First 3 secondsStart with energy. The hook determines if people watch.
CaptionsAdd text captions in editing. 85% watch without sound.
Trending AudioUse trending sounds on TikTok/Reels when they fit your content.

Cross-Posting Workflow

  1. Film once in vertical (9:16)
  2. Edit with captions in CapCut or InShot
  3. Post to Instagram Reels first
  4. Download without watermark, post to TikTok
  5. Post same video to YouTube Shorts
  6. Share Reel to Facebook

Total extra time: ~5 minutes per video for 3x the reach.

UGC Strategy

User-generated content gets 4.5x more engagement than branded content. Here's how to collect it:

Ask customers to use #GarberChevyMidland or #MyGarberChevy when posting their new car.

Incentive Program

  • Best customer photo of the month = free oil change

  • Review on Google = small gift (air freshener, keychain)

How to Use UGC

  • Repost customer photos/videos to Stories (ask permission first)
  • Create "Customer Spotlight" posts
  • Screenshot positive comments for social proof
