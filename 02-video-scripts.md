## Quick Tips for Filming

1. **Lighting** - Face a window or be outside. Never backlit.
2. **Audio** - Quiet location. Wind kills audio.
3. **Energy** - First 3 seconds need ENERGY. Start strong.
4. **Eye contact** - Look at camera lens, not screen.
5. **One take** - Don't overthink. Authentic > perfect.
6. **Captions** - Add them in editing. 85% watch muted.
