## Executive Summary

This document provides a complete social media video content strategy focused on **customer education** rather than industry humor. Research shows this approach drives significantly higher engagement and actual car sales.

**Key Statistics:**
- 95% of car buyers start their search online
- 80% watch video before visiting a dealership
- 76% have purchased based on social media content
- Video viewers are 1.81x more likely to purchase

**Platforms:** Instagram Reels + Facebook
**Frequency:** 5-7 videos per week
**Content Focus:** Hidden Features, Tips, Customer Deliveries

### Script 7: Winter Tire Pressure Myth
**Length:** 30 seconds | **Type:** Tips

**[0-3 sec] HOOK:**
> "Your tire pressure light came on this morning? Here's what's actually happening."

**[3-22 sec] CONTENT:**
> "Cold air is DENSER than warm air. So when temperatures drop, your tire pressure drops too - about 1 PSI for every 10 degrees. That doesn't mean you have a leak. It's just physics. Check your pressure when tires are COLD, fill to the number on your door jamb - NOT on the tire."

**[22-27 sec] PAYOFF:**
> "Do this once and that light goes away."

**[27-30 sec] CTA:**
> "Share with someone who panics every winter."

## Filming Tips

**Equipment:** iPhone is sufficient. No professional gear needed.

**Lighting:** Face natural light (window or outdoors). Never backlit.

**Audio:** Quiet location. Wind ruins audio.

**Format:** Vertical (9:16) for Reels

**First 3 seconds:** Start with energy. The hook determines if people watch.

**Captions:** Add text captions in editing. 85% watch without sound.
